About WTools

WTools is a powerful and user-friendly tool designed to enhance your messaging and media management experience. It helps you stay on top of your communication while simplifying content management. Key features include:

message Automatic Reply

Set up custom triggers to send automatic responses based on keywords or exact matches. Perfect for staying connected even when you're busy!

message Direct Messaging

Send messages directly to any contact without needing to save their number.

videocam Video Trimmer

Trim videos to the perfect length, ideal for sharing as status updates or keeping your clips concise.

download Status Downloader

Effortlessly download images and videos shared as status updates and save them to your device for future use.

photo_library Media Manager

Organize and manage all your photos, videos, and media files with ease.
