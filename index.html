About WTools

WTools is a powerful and user-friendly tool designed to make your messaging and media management simpler and more efficient. With a range of features to enhance your communication and content management, this app offers:

messageDirect Messaging

Send messages directly to any contact without needing to save their number.

videocamVideo Trimmer

Trim videos to the perfect length, ideal for sharing as status updates or keeping your clips concise.

downloadStatus Downloader

Effortlessly download images and videos shared as status updates and save them to your device for future use.

photo_libraryMedia Manager

Organize and manage all your photos, videos, and media files with ease.
