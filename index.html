About WTools

WTools is a powerful and user-friendly tool designed to make your messaging and media management simpler and more efficient. With a range of features to enhance your communication and content management, this app offers:

  • Direct Messaging – Send messages directly to any contact without needing to save their number.
  • Video Trimmer – Trim videos to the perfect length, ideal for sharing as status updates.
  • Status Downloader – Effortlessly download images and videos shared as status updates.
  • Media Manager – Easily manage all your media content (photos, videos, etc.).
